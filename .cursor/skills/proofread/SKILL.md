# Proofread Skill

## Overview

Thorough proofreading for grammar, typos, and consistency.

## What to Check

### 1. Grammar and Spelling
- Subject-verb agreement
- Tense consistency
- Spelling errors
- Punctuation

### 2. Style
- Passive voice (minimize)
- Hedging language (be precise)
- Jargon (define or simplify)
- Sentence length (vary)

### 3. Consistency
- Notation (same symbol = same meaning)
- Terminology (pick one term, stick to it)
- Formatting (consistent throughout)
- Citations (consistent style)

### 4. Academic Writing
- Claims supported by evidence
- Hedging appropriate to certainty
- Topic sentences clear
- Transitions smooth

## Severity Levels

- **Critical:** Math errors, broken citations, factual errors
- **Major:** Grammar errors, unclear sentences
- **Minor:** Style preferences, minor inconsistencies
